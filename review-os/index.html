Review OS

A GitHub-based study system that turns notes, mistakes, writing materials, daily practice, and AI handoff rules into a maintainable learning project.

Project idea

Review OS is not just a folder of notes. It is a structured workflow for keeping learning materials traceable, reusable, and ready for future AI-assisted maintenance.

Layers

  • Vault: source notes and attachments.
  • Models: reusable problem-solving patterns.
  • Writing: essay materials, drafts, and adaptation rules.
  • Agent: handoff rules for AI tools.
  • Deploy: public pages and reusable templates.

Why it matters

A study period ends, but the system can remain. The repository becomes a reusable prototype for AI-assisted review workflows.

Project brief

Repo as Review OS: a deployable AI-assisted learning system.
