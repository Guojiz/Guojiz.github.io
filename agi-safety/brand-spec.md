# Guojiz Research Brand Spec

## Identity

- Chinese name: 郭己正
- English name: Guojiz
- Chinese descriptor: 独立人工智能研究
- English descriptor: Independent AI Research
- Primary thesis line: 能力可以增长。主权不得转移。
- English thesis line: Capability can grow. Sovereignty must not transfer.

The name is the identity. The site does not use an invented symbol or substitute logo.

## Color Roles

- Night: `#0B0D0C`
- Paper: `#F1EDE4`
- Stage text: `#F0E7D2`
- Human purpose: `#F2A65A`
- ASI capability: `#59B7CE`
- Mirror termination capability / deterrence boundary: `#C84B35`

Colors carry fixed semantic roles throughout the particle narrative and research document.

## Typography

- Display: Iowan Old Style / Baskerville / Songti SC / STSong / SimSun / Georgia
- Interface and body: Avenir Next / PingFang SC / Microsoft YaHei / Segoe UI
- No externally hosted font is required.

## Motion

- Vertical scroll remains the primary narrative control.
- Arrow keys, Page Up/Down, Home/End, and Space provide accessible scene stepping without replacing scroll.
- The same particle identities persist through every scene.
- Motion explains a change in authority, access, process, or continuity.
- Scroll progress uses time-based damping, cubic in-out easing, and deterministic per-particle stagger rather than frame-dependent snapping.
- A silent failure echo briefly removes ASI and mirror particles while human particles remain; it is an implication, not a labeled chapter.
- Camera spectacle, planetary imagery, and decorative particle motion are excluded.
- Reduced-motion mode preserves every scene and claim while reducing shimmer and transition lag.

## Layout

- Animated argument: full-viewport dark stage with side copy.
- Full research: light editorial document after the animation.
- Eight-pixel spacing base.
- Sharp edges, hairline dividers, no decorative card shadows, no glass panels.

## Language

- Chinese and English are complete, switchable versions of the same site.
- English is the default.
- The language control shows only the alternate language: `中文` on English pages and `English` on Chinese pages.
- Chinese uses 郭己正; English uses Guojiz.
- Navigation, particle narration, document content, metadata, and title all follow the selected language.

## Research Voice

- Present propositions as research questions until they survive literature, formal-model, and counterexample review.
- Invite narrow criticism and literature guidance before asking for endorsement or co-authorship.
- State the July 2026 phase-one launch clearly without implying that the framework is already validated.
- Collaboration language should be intellectually open, specific about needed fields, and free of prestige claims.
