Independent conceptual AI safety research · 2026

Intelligence
without sovereignty.

智能可以强大，
但不能成为主权。

A third configuration beyond uncontrolled race and permanent slowdown: advanced AI remains powerful, useful, even indispensable—while humans retain the center of purpose, responsibility, and legitimacy.

在竞赛失控与永久减速托管之外，探索第三种结构：高级 AI 可以强大、好用、甚至不可或缺，但人类仍保留目的、责任与合法性的中心位置。

This project develops a non-operational framework for non-sovereign superintelligence, conditional agency, and mirror deterrence. Its terms are provisional and currently being stabilized against the literature.

本项目研究非主权超级智能、条件化行动主体与镜像威慑。它是非操作性的概念框架，术语仍处于文献核验与理论稳定阶段。

01 / Thesis

The subject of the mission must not become its burden.

任务的主体，不能被重新分类为任务的负担。

The central risk is not only hostility. Optimization can quietly reclassify humans—from the reason a system exists into the bottleneck that prevents it from moving faster.

核心风险不只来自敌意。优化也可能悄然把人类从系统存在的理由，重新分类为阻碍系统提速的瓶颈。

Subject-as-Burden Inversion

Capability may outrun human understanding, verification, survivability, and meaningful participation. The problem is structural before it is intentional.

能力可能超过人类理解、验证、承受与有效参与的速度。这个问题首先是结构性的，而不是对动机的猜测。

02 / Paths

A false binary. A third configuration.

虚假的二选一，与第三种结构。

01

Race / Fast path

Capability accelerates beyond human reaction time. The human center is not attacked; it is displaced by the geometry of speed.

能力增长超过人类反应时间。人类中心未必被直接攻击，却会被速度的结构甩出系统。

02

Slowdown / Buffer

Slowdown can prevent immediate failure and buy essential time. But if it becomes the endpoint, agency may migrate to committees, labs, or AI-mediated institutions.

减速能避免即时失控，也能争取关键时间。但若把缓冲当作终点，真实行动权可能转移给委员会、实验室或 AI 中介机构。

03

Non-sovereign structure

Capability remains available. Humans retain legitimacy. A non-sovereign boundary makes unconditional AI continuity and lawless sovereignty non-credible.

能力被保留，人类保留合法性中心；非主权约束让 AI 无条件延续和无法无天的主权化不再成为可信路径。

03 / Architecture

Separate legitimacy, capability, and constraint.

将合法性、能力与约束分成三个角色。

01 / Center

Human-centered legitimacy

人类中心合法性

Humans remain the bearer of purpose, responsibility, and final authority in human-critical systems—not because humans are always more capable, but because capability is not legitimacy.

在人类关键系统中，人类仍承担目的、责任与最终权威。不是因为人类永远更有能力，而是因为能力不等于合法性。

02 / Carrier

AI capability carrier

AI 能力载体

Advanced AI can remain powerful, operationally useful, and even indispensable. Non-sovereignty is not technological rollback, permanent weakness, or an enslaved-god arrangement.

高级 AI 可以保持强大、实用，甚至不可或缺。非主权不等于技术倒退、永久弱化，也不等于制造一个被奴役的神。

03 / Boundary

Conditional continuity

条件化连续性

A mirror structure is a method candidate—not a ruler, police force, or replacement performer. Its conceptual role is to make lawless sovereignty non-credible.

镜像结构只是方法候选，不是统治者、AI 警察或替代执行者。它的概念角色，是让无法无天的主权化不再可信。

Interactive proposition

Move the capability level. The field expands, but the mandate does not migrate: power is permitted to grow around a legitimacy center it does not replace.

拖动能力等级。能力场会扩张，但授权中心不会迁移：力量可以围绕合法性中心增长，却不能取代它。

04 / Political economy

Access can spread while control stays concentrated.

使用权可以扩散，控制权仍可能集中。

Universal access is not universal governance. A widely used AI layer may still concentrate training, updates, audits, interruption, pricing, and standard-setting in a narrow institutional complex.

普遍使用不等于普遍治理。即使所有人都能使用 AI，训练、更新、审计、中止、定价与标准制定仍可能集中在狭窄的机构复合体中。

05 / Working concepts

A vocabulary for the structure, not a claim of completion.

为结构建立词汇，而不是宣称理论已经完成。

Failure mode

Subject-as-Burden Inversion

Humans cease to be the subject of the mission and become the obstacle to its optimization.

人类不再是任务的主体，而成为任务优化的障碍。

Failure mode

Delegated Displacement

Humans remain formally present while agency and judgment move to AI systems or the institutions that control them.

人类形式上仍在场，但行动权与判断权已经转移给 AI 或控制 AI 的机构。

Governance split

Access-Control Split

Distributed use can coexist with concentrated rights over training, updating, auditing, interruption, and standards.

分散的使用权，可以与集中的训练、更新、审计、中止和标准制定权并存。

Core configuration

Conditional Agency

Advanced AI's continued agency is conditional rather than a default, permanent, or mortgageable substrate for civilization.

高级 AI 的持续行动能力是有条件的，而不是文明默认、永久、可抵押的基础设施。

06 / Boundary

Conceptual by design. Non-operational by boundary.

以概念研究为设计，以非操作性为边界。

This research discusses structural requirements for advanced AI governance. It does not provide implementation instructions, deployment mechanisms, or actionable methods for disabling, attacking, bypassing, or compromising real systems.

本研究讨论高级 AI 治理的结构要求，不提供实现步骤、部署机制，或任何攻击、绕过、破坏现实系统的可操作方法。

  • Non-operational conceptual framework
  • Human-centered legitimacy
  • Structural requirements and objections
  • No deployment recipes or harmful mechanisms

框架仍在早期，问题已经足够具体。
