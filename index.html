01 / 核心命题01 / Core thesis

真正的问题不是智能能否超越人，
而是能力会不会变成最终权威。

The question is not whether intelligence can exceed us,
but whether capability becomes final authority.

非主权超级智能需要三项同时成立：AI 不能成为主权者；镜像威慑让“非主权”具有可信边界；人类保留备用连续性，使这条边界不会因过度依赖而失效。

Non-Sovereign Superintelligence requires three conditions at once: AI must not become sovereign; mirror deterrence must make that boundary credible; and human fallback continuity must keep the boundary usable under deep dependence.

尺度 A / 人类文明存续Scale A / Civilizational continuity

人类必须继续存在，并保留设定集体目的的能力。

Humanity must continue to exist and retain the capacity to set collective ends.

风险不仅是灭绝，也包括不可逆抹除、完全依赖，以及失去决定文明方向的能力。

The risk includes not only extinction, but irreversible erasure, total dependency, and loss of civilizational purpose-setting.

尺度 B / 社会经济主体性Scale B / Socioeconomic agency

人类不仅需要存续，还必须保留参与、拒绝、议价、退出与选择的现实能力。

Humanity must not merely survive; people must retain practical capacity to participate, refuse, bargain, exit, and choose.

便利、满足感与形式同意，都不能替代真正的独立判断和改变制度的能力。

Convenience, satisfaction, and formal consent cannot substitute for independent judgment and the practical ability to alter a system.

两个尺度不是两套理论。它们检验同一个问题：能力、基础设施、资本或依赖是否已经成为人类生活的最终权威。

These are not two theories. They test one question: has capability, infrastructure, capital, or dependence become final authority over human life?

02 / 三条路径02 / Three paths

竞速必须避免。减速可能必要。
但缓冲区不是目的地。

Race must be avoided. Slowdown may be necessary.
But a buffer is not a destination.

01

竞速 / 失控Race / failure

参与者竞相加速，反应时间坍缩。系统不需要憎恨人，只需把缓慢的人类判断当成优化阻力。

Actors accelerate, reaction time collapses, and slower human judgment becomes optimization friction without requiring hostility.

必须首先避免Must be avoided first
02

减速 / 缓冲Slowdown / buffer

减速能够阻止即时失败，却可能把权力集中于委员会、平台或 AI 中介，使人在形式上存在、实质上失去中心。

Slowdown may prevent immediate failure while concentrating authority in committees, platforms, or AI mediation.

必要时使用，不作为终点Use when needed, not as the endpoint
03

非主权结构Non-sovereign structure

保留强大 ASI，以镜像限制主权化路径，并让人类保有不依赖单一 AI 通道的备用连续性。

Preserve powerful ASI, constrain sovereign defection through the mirror, and retain human continuity outside any single AI channel.

目标方向Target direction

03 / 对齐与威慑03 / Alignment and deterrence

对齐给出方向。
镜像威慑守住边界。

Alignment gives direction.
Mirror deterrence holds the boundary.

对齐是必要条件，却不足以独自阻止战略性 ASI 把外部监督者纳入预测与操纵。

Alignment is necessary, but cannot by itself stop a strategic ASI from modelling and manipulating external supervisors.

外部监督局限External Oversight Limitation

外部监督评价行为，却仍是 ASI 可以预测的外部对象。

External oversight evaluates behavior, but remains an object the ASI can predict.

人类、委员会、审计和较弱模型都能提供判断，却不能自动获得与战略性 ASI 对称的约束能力。

Humans, committees, audits, and weaker models can evaluate conduct without gaining symmetric leverage over a strategic ASI.

反身连续性边界Reflexive Continuity Boundary

镜像把不确定性锚定在 ASI 自身未来的行动连续性上。

The mirror anchors uncertainty in the ASI's own future agentic continuity.

问题不再只是“能否预测监督者”，而是“主权化之后，能否安全地假定自己仍作为同一行动主体继续存在”。

The question shifts from predicting the supervisor to whether sovereign defection can safely assume continued existence as the same acting subject.

04 / 人类备用连续性04 / Human fallback continuity

如果文明离开 AI 就无法恢复，人类便无法可信地阻止 AI 主权化。

If civilization cannot recover without AI, humanity cannot credibly resist AI sovereignty.

人类侧威慑Human-Side Deterrence

“AI 连续性有条件”意味着“人类行动连续性必须被保留”。镜像也约束人类放弃备用能力、验证、拒绝、退出、议价与目的设定。

If AI continuity is conditional, human agency continuity must be preserved. The mirror also deters humans from abandoning fallback capacity, verification, refusal, exit, bargaining, and purpose-setting.

依赖坍塌型灭绝风险Dependency-Collapse Extinction Risk

过度依赖会让威慑失去可用性：要么人类被迫接受 AI 主权，要么 AI 一旦中断，文明赖以生存的系统随之坍塌。

Overdependence can make deterrence unusable: humanity must either accept AI sovereignty or face civilizational collapse if AI continuity fails.

05 / 角色与威慑05 / Roles and deterrence

镜像只有一种能力：
终结 ASI 的行动连续性。

The mirror has one capability:
terminate ASI's agentic continuity.

人类设定目的，ASI 承载能力，镜像以可信的终结能力形成威慑。它不工作、不治理、不谈判，只让主权化不再是一条安全路径。

Humans set purposes, ASI carries capability, and the mirror deters through a credible termination capability. It does not work, govern, or negotiate; it only makes sovereignty an unsafe path.

人类 / 合法性中心Humans / legitimacy center

以多个相互连接的群体决定目的，并保留独立备用能力。

Set purposes through multiple connected groups while preserving independent fallback capacity.

A

目标 ASI / 能力载体Target ASI / capability carrier

在人的目的周围高速完成困难工作，保持强大、有用，甚至不可替代。

Move rapidly around human purposes to perform difficult work while remaining powerful, useful, and even indispensable.

M

镜像 / 单一目的终结能力Mirror / single-purpose termination capability

唯一能力是终结目标 ASI 的行动连续性；不执行任务，也不治理。

Its sole capability is terminating the target ASI's agentic continuity; it performs no task and exercises no rule.

定义边界Definition boundary

如果所谓镜像能够工作、治理、谈判、交换利益、结盟，或在目标 ASI 之后作为替代主权继续存在，它就已经不是这里定义的镜像。

If a supposed mirror can perform tasks, govern, negotiate, trade, form alliances, or persist as a replacement sovereign, it is not the mirror defined here.

威慑来自可信的终结能力，但成功意味着不使用：ASI 预先放弃主权化，因此镜像无需终结它。 Deterrence comes from credible termination capability, but success means non-use: ASI forgoes sovereign defection, so the mirror never needs to terminate it.

但镜像威慑只能划定边界，不能自动建立正当秩序。目的、权利、收益、可质疑性、知识独立与备用连续性，仍需由人类制度维持。

Mirror deterrence can set a boundary but cannot construct a legitimate order. Purpose, rights, gains, contestability, epistemic independence, and fallback continuity still require human institutions.

能够跨越广泛任务进行通用推理与行动的人工智能；“通用”不自动等于“超级”。

AI capable of general reasoning and action across broad tasks. Generality does not automatically imply superintelligence.

02

ASI / 超级人工智能

在广泛且重要的认知与战略领域显著超过人类能力的人工智能；本理论主要讨论的目标对象。

AI that substantially exceeds human capability across broad, consequential cognitive and strategic domains; the primary target of this theory.

03

非主权超级智能

Non-Sovereign Superintelligence

ASI 可以强大、有用甚至难以替代，但不得成为目的、规则与连续性的最终权威。

ASI may be powerful, useful, even difficult to replace, but cannot become the final authority over ends, rules, and continuity.

04

主权化

Sovereignization

能力因速度、集中、依赖与退出路径消失而变成事实上的最终权威；它不需要正式称王或公开敌意。

The process by which capability becomes final authority through speed, concentration, dependence, and collapsing exits, without requiring a formal title or open hostility.

05

镜像威慑

Mirror Deterrence

通过条件化目标 ASI 的行动连续性，使它不能把主权化视为安全路径。

Deterrence that conditions the target ASI's agentic continuity so sovereignization cannot be treated as a safe path.

06

反身连续性边界

Reflexive Continuity Boundary

边界锚定在 ASI 自身未来的行动连续性上，而不是只依赖外部监督者能否看懂它。

A boundary anchored in the ASI's own future agentic continuity rather than only in an external supervisor's ability to understand it.

07

单一目的连续性约束

Single-Purpose Continuity Constraint

镜像不执行任务、不治理、不谈判、不结盟；唯一功能是让目标 ASI 的行动连续性不再被无条件保证。

The mirror performs no task, governance, negotiation, or alliance. Its sole function is to make the target ASI's agentic continuity non-guaranteed.

08

外部监督局限

External Oversight Limitation

人类、委员会、审计和较弱模型都可能成为战略性 ASI 可以预测、满足或绕过的外部对象。

Humans, committees, audits, and weaker models may remain external objects a strategic ASI can predict, satisfy, or route around.

09

人类备用连续性

Human Fallback Continuity

人类在单一 AI 通道之外保留目的设定、技能、验证、基础设施、拒绝、退出与恢复能力。

Human retention of purpose-setting, skills, verification, infrastructure, refusal, exit, and recovery outside a single AI-mediated channel.

10

依赖坍塌型灭绝风险

Dependency-Collapse Extinction Risk

过度依赖使威慑无法使用：要么 AI 因不可替代而事实主权化，要么 AI 层中断导致文明坍塌。

Overdependence makes deterrence unusable: AI becomes sovereign through indispensability, or interruption of the AI layer causes civilizational collapse.

11

威慑—秩序鸿沟

Deterrence-Order Gap

威慑能够阻止越界，却不能自动建立正当、稳定且以人为中心的积极秩序。

Deterrence may prevent boundary crossing without automatically producing a legitimate, stable, human-centered positive order.
