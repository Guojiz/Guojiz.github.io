Independent AI governance research direction · Phase 0

Capability can grow.
Sovereignty must not transfer.

能力可以增长，
主权不能转移。

An early research proposition: advanced AI may become powerful, useful, even indispensable—without becoming the final center of purpose, responsibility, and legitimacy.

一个尚在起步阶段的研究命题：高级 AI 可以强大、好用、甚至不可或缺，却不应成为目的、责任与合法性的最终中心。

Research in formation研究正在形成

This is not a completed theory, a forecast, or a deployment design. The site makes the starting question visible while the literature, objections, and formal structure are still being built.

这不是已经完成的理论、未来预测或部署方案。网站只把起点问题公开出来；文献、反驳与形式结构仍待后续研究。

01 / Starting question

What must remain human when intelligence no longer is?

当智能不再是人类专属，什么仍必须属于人类？

The project begins from a distinction: greater capability does not, by itself, create legitimate authority over the people and purposes that capability is meant to serve.

研究从一个区分开始：能力更强，并不会自动获得支配其所服务的人类与目的的合法权威。

Starting observation / 起点观察

The concern is structural before it is intentional: speed, dependence, delegation, and concentrated control may displace human agency even without a hostile AI.

这首先是结构问题，而不是动机猜测：即使 AI 没有敌意，速度、依赖、委托和控制集中也可能让人类失去行动位置。

Scope / 已确定 Conceptual AI governance research AI 治理概念研究

Non-operational, public-facing, and bounded away from harmful implementation details.

保持非操作性与公开可讨论性，不进入危险实现细节。

Proposition / 工作命题 Powerful AI need not be sovereign AI 强 AI 不必成为主权 AI

This is the proposition to investigate, not a conclusion already demonstrated.

这是要研究的命题，不是已经被证明的结论。

Unknown / 尚未解决 What makes the separation credible? 怎样让这种分离真正可信？

Institutional, legal, technical, and philosophical answers remain open.

制度、法律、技术与哲学层面的答案仍然开放。

02 / Problem framing

Two familiar paths. One question still open.

两条熟悉的路径，一个仍待回答的问题。

01

Race / Failure risk

If capability accelerates beyond human understanding and reaction time, people may be displaced by speed and dependence—not necessarily by explicit hostility.

如果能力增长超过人类理解与反应速度，人类可能因速度和依赖而失去位置，并不需要先假设 AI 具有明确敌意。

02

Slowdown / Buffer

Slowing down may be necessary to buy time. The open concern is whether a temporary buffer can quietly become permanent management by a narrow set of institutions.

减速可能是争取时间的必要手段。尚待研究的是：临时缓冲会不会悄然变成少数机构的永久托管。

03

Third-path question

Can advanced capability remain available while purpose, responsibility, and final legitimacy stay human-centered? “Non-sovereign superintelligence” is the working name for that inquiry.

能否保留高级能力，同时让目的、责任和最终合法性仍以人类为中心？“非主权超级智能”是这个研究问题的暂定名称。

Conceptual state study · not a forecastCapability ≠ legitimacy

03 / Provisional model

Three roles to distinguish before designing answers.

先区分三个角色，再谈如何设计答案。

Open question 01

What remains non-delegable?

什么不能被委托？

Which purposes, responsibilities, and decisions must remain answerable to humans even when machines perform the difficult work better?

即使机器更擅长完成困难工作，哪些目的、责任与决定仍必须向人类负责？

Open question 02

How can capability remain useful?

怎样保留真正的能力？

A third path cannot simply rename permanent weakness or technological rollback. It must take the usefulness and possible indispensability of strong AI seriously.

第三条路不能只是给永久弱化或技术倒退换一个名字；它必须认真面对强 AI 的实用性，以及它可能变得不可或缺。

Open question 03

What prevents sovereignty drift?

什么阻止主权漂移？

What combination of institutions, contestability, resilience, and constraints can stop capability or infrastructure dependence from becoming final authority?

怎样组合制度、可争议性、韧性与约束，才能阻止能力或基础设施依赖转化为最终权威？

04 / Structural question

Does wider access actually distribute power?

更多人能使用，真的等于权力更分散吗？

A possibility to investigate: access may spread while control over training, updates, audits, interruption, pricing, and standards remains concentrated. The distinction matters; its real-world extent requires evidence.

一个需要研究的可能性是：使用权可以扩散，而训练、更新、审计、中止、定价与标准制定权仍然集中。这个区分很重要，但现实程度必须由证据回答。

05 / Vocabulary in formation

Names for questions the research still has to earn.

先给问题命名，再用研究让这些名字站得住。

Provisional term

Subject-as-Burden Inversion

A working label for the risk that humans shift from being the purpose of a system to being treated as friction against its optimization.

暂时用来描述一种风险：人类从系统服务的目的，变成系统优化眼中的阻力。

Provisional term

Delegated Displacement

A working label for formal human presence alongside the gradual migration of real judgment and agency elsewhere.

暂时用来描述：人类形式上仍在场，但真实判断权与行动权逐渐迁移到别处。

Provisional term

Access-Control Split

A distinction to test between who can use an AI system and who can train, update, audit, interrupt, and govern it.

一个需要验证的区分：谁能使用 AI，与谁能训练、更新、审计、中止和治理它，并不是同一件事。

Research direction

Non-Sovereign Superintelligence

The umbrella question: can intelligence become extremely capable without acquiring final authority over human-critical purposes and institutions?

总问题：智能能否变得极其强大，却不获得对人类关键目的与制度的最终权威？

06 / Boundary

Conceptual by design. Non-operational by boundary.

以概念研究为设计，以非操作性为边界。

This research discusses structural requirements for advanced AI governance. It does not provide implementation instructions, deployment mechanisms, or actionable methods for disabling, attacking, bypassing, or compromising real systems.

本研究讨论高级 AI 治理的结构要求，不提供实现步骤、部署机制，或任何攻击、绕过、破坏现实系统的可操作方法。

  • Non-operational conceptual framework
  • Human-centered legitimacy
  • Structural requirements and objections
  • No deployment recipes or harmful mechanisms

07 / What happens next

The research has not begun in earnest. This is the starting line.

研究还没有真正展开。这里是起跑线。

The public site should make the question legible without pretending that terminology, evidence, or mechanisms are settled. Future work will determine which parts survive contact with the literature and serious objections.

这个公开网站只负责把问题讲清楚，不假装术语、证据或机制已经成熟。后续研究将决定哪些想法能经受文献与严肃反驳的检验。

  1. Build and verify the literature map.建立并核验文献地图。
  2. Separate philosophical claims from governance and method candidates.区分哲学命题、治理方向与方法候选。
  3. Develop the strongest objections before expanding the theory.在扩展理论前，先建立最有力的反驳。
