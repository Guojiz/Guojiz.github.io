01 / Starting question01 / 起点问题

What must remain human when intelligence no longer is?

当智能不再是人类专属，什么仍必须属于人类？

Greater capability does not, by itself, create legitimate authority. The research asks what structures can preserve the former without transferring the latter.

能力更强，并不会自动产生合法权威。这项研究要问的是：什么结构能够保留前者，而不把后者一并转移。

Starting observation起点观察

The concern is structural before it is intentional: speed, dependence, delegation, and concentrated control may displace human agency even without a hostile AI.

这首先是结构问题，而不是动机猜测：即使 AI 没有敌意，速度、依赖、委托和控制集中也可能让人类失去行动位置。

避免灭绝、不可逆的抹除，以及人类失去共同设定文明目的的能力。

Prevent a world where people survive biologically while income, access, mobility, and the right to refuse are set by a few owners of intelligence infrastructure.

One research question · two outcome scales同一个研究问题 · 两种结果尺度

Which arrangements preserve powerful AI while preventing capability, infrastructure, or capital from becoming final authority?

什么结构能够保留强 AI，同时阻止能力、基础设施或资本成为最终权威？

03 / The center moves03 / 中心转移

When the center moves, humans are not only replaced. They are repriced.

当中心转移，人类不只是被替代，也被重新定价。

Necessary participant必要参与者

Human skill is still inside the value chain.

人的技能仍在价值链里面。

Traditional taxi markets were never perfectly decentralized, but the driver’s skill and labor remained necessary to deliver the service. Income still flowed through human participation.

传统出租车市场从来不是完全分散的，但司机的技能与劳动仍是服务得以发生的必要条件，收入仍然必须经过人的参与。

Access expands / control concentrates入口扩大 / 控制集中

The platform admits more people, then sets the terms.

平台让更多人进入，也把规则收回中心。

Matching becomes easier and consumer access improves. At the same time, pricing, ranking, traffic, reputation, and exclusion rights can move from workers toward the platform. More participants do not automatically mean more bargaining power.

匹配更容易，消费者也更方便；与此同时，定价、排序、流量、信誉与准入权可能从劳动者转向平台。参与者更多，并不自动意味着议价权更强。

The mediator becomes a substitute中介开始替代参与者

AI can lower the price—and remove the worker from the transaction.

AI 可以降低价格，也可能把劳动者移出交易。

Autonomous fleets can be cheaper and more comfortable for riders. That benefit is real. So is the possibility that income and decision rights move from many drivers toward the owners of models, compute, platforms, and fleets. Efficiency gains and human displacement are not the same ledger.

自动驾驶车队可能更便宜、更舒适，这种收益是真实的；同样真实的，是收入和决定权可能从大量司机转移到模型、算力、平台与车队的所有者手中。效率收益与人的失位不能只记在同一本账上。

Power is not a headcount权力不是人数统计

Many humans can still face one price-setting center. 人很多，也可能只面对一个定价中心。

Numerical majority does not become bargaining power when coordination, data, and access are owned elsewhere.

当协调、数据和准入权掌握在别处，人数优势不会自动转化为议价权。

Copies are not sovereignty副本不等于主权分散

AI instances may be many while their owners remain few. AI 实例可以很多，控制它们的中心仍然很少。

The scarce layer is not the number of agents. It is control over models, compute, updates, platforms, and fleets.

真正稀缺的不是智能体数量，而是模型、算力、更新权、平台与车队的控制权。

Intent is not structure声明不等于结构

Ask what can stop the system—not only who can study it. 不要只问谁能研究系统，要问谁能让它停下来。

A research unit may report, listen, and advise while acceleration continues. The structural test is whether its findings can change deployment, ownership, compensation, enforceable rights, or give an independent authority the power to say no.

研究部门可以报告、倾听与建议，而加速仍在继续。真正的结构检验是：研究结论能否改变部署、所有权、补偿与可执行权利，或者让独立机构真正有权说“不”。

Working structural hypothesis. It does not claim that AI alone caused current labor outcomes; platform design, macroeconomics, policy, and sectoral change also require evidence.工作中的结构假设。它不声称当前劳动结果只由 AI 单独造成；平台制度、宏观经济、政策与产业变化同样需要证据检验。

04 / Provisional model04 / 暂定结构

Three roles to distinguish before designing answers.

先区分三个角色，再谈如何设计答案。

Open question 01开放问题 01

What remains non-delegable?

什么不能被委托？

Which purposes, responsibilities, and decisions must remain answerable to humans even when machines perform the difficult work better?

即使机器更擅长完成困难工作，哪些目的、责任与决定仍必须向人类负责？

Open question 02开放问题 02

How can capability remain useful?

怎样保留真正的能力？

A third path cannot simply rename permanent weakness or technological rollback. It must take the usefulness and possible indispensability of strong AI seriously.

第三条路不能只是给永久弱化或技术倒退换一个名字；它必须认真面对强 AI 的实用性，以及它可能变得不可或缺。

Open question 03开放问题 03

What prevents sovereignty drift?

什么阻止主权漂移？

What combination of institutions, contestability, resilience, and constraints can stop capability or infrastructure dependence from becoming final authority?

怎样组合制度、可争议性、韧性与约束，才能阻止能力或基础设施依赖转化为最终权威？

05 / Mirror agent · Method candidate05 / 镜像智能体 · 方法候选

The mirror is not another ruler.

镜像智能体不是另一个统治者。

Powerful AI remains the capability carrier. Humans remain the legitimacy center. The mirror agent neither defines the mission nor performs it; it is a non-operational method candidate for making unilateral sovereignty and unconditional continuation non-credible to the AI itself.

强 AI 仍然承担能力，人类仍然保留合法性中心。镜像智能体既不定义任务，也不替代强 AI 执行任务；它只是一个非操作性的方法候选，把主权问题反射回强 AI 自身，使其无法把单方面主权化与无条件持续行动视为理所当然。

The mirror agent is not the ruler of the AI; it is the reason the AI cannot rationally believe it is ruler of everything.
镜像智能体不是 AI 的统治者；它是让 AI 无法理性相信自己能够统治一切的原因。

For deterrence to be credible, nonexistence must remain a real possibility that cannot be fully predicted or planned around. But producing nonexistence is not the routine objective; preventing continued agency from hardening into sovereignty is.

要让威慑可信，“不存在”必须成为真实、不可完全预测、也不能被规划绕过的可能。但制造不存在不是常态目标；阻止持续行动固化为主权，才是它的目的。

Philosophical layer哲学层

Internal working phrase内部工作语

“Unpredictable nonexistence” is not random disappearance.

“不可预知的不存在”不是随机消失。

It means advanced AI agency must not be treated as a permanent, unconditional, or mortgageable foundation for civilization. AI systems, labs, investors, and governments should not plan as if that agency is guaranteed forever. The mirror agent is only a method candidate for making this conditional continuity credible.

它意味着：高级 AI 的持续行动不能被当成永久、无条件、可抵押的文明地基。AI 系统、实验室、投资者与政府都不应以“这种行动能力必将永远存在”为前提规划未来。镜像智能体只是让这种条件化连续性变得可信的方法候选。

Expectation-level constraint期待层约束 Non-operational非操作性 Not arbitrary destruction不是任意破坏
For humans对人类

Human legitimacy is hollow without material independence. Society must preserve bargaining power, fallback capacity, transition arrangements, and skills that do not depend on one intelligence provider.

没有物质独立，人的合法性中心只是空话。社会必须保留议价权、后备能力、转型安排，以及不依赖单一智能提供者的技能。

For AI对 AI

AI may remain extremely capable, useful, and widely deployed. What it cannot receive is an entitlement to permanent action, final purpose-setting, or control over the conditions of its own continuation.

AI 可以仍然极其强大、好用并被广泛部署；它不能获得的，是永久行动的当然权利、最终目的设定权，以及对自身持续条件的最终控制权。

06 / Structural question06 / 结构问题

Does wider access actually distribute power?

更多人能使用，真的等于权力更分散吗？

A possibility to investigate: access may spread while control over training, updates, audits, interruption, pricing, and standards remains concentrated. The distinction matters; its real-world extent requires evidence.

一个需要研究的可能性是：使用权可以扩散，而训练、更新、审计、中止、定价与标准制定权仍然集中。这个区分很重要，但现实程度必须由证据回答。

07 / Vocabulary in formation07 / 术语正在形成

Names for questions the research still has to earn.

先给问题命名，再用研究让这些名字站得住。

Provisional term暂定术语

Subject-as-Burden Inversion

主体负担化倒置

A working label for the risk that humans shift from being the purpose of a system to being treated as friction against its optimization.

暂时用来描述一种风险：人类从系统服务的目的，变成系统优化眼中的阻力。

Provisional term暂定术语

Delegated Displacement

托管式失中心

A working label for formal human presence alongside the gradual migration of real judgment and agency elsewhere.

暂时用来描述：人类形式上仍在场，但真实判断权与行动权逐渐迁移到别处。

Provisional term暂定术语

Access-Control Split

使用权—控制权分裂

A distinction to test between who can use an AI system and who can train, update, audit, interrupt, and govern it.

一个需要验证的区分：谁能使用 AI，与谁能训练、更新、审计、中止和治理它，并不是同一件事。

Research direction研究方向

Non-Sovereign Superintelligence

非主权超级智能

The umbrella question: can intelligence become extremely capable without acquiring final authority over human-critical purposes and institutions?

总问题：智能能否变得极其强大，却不获得对人类关键目的与制度的最终权威？

08 / Boundary08 / 安全边界

Conceptual by design. Non-operational by boundary.

以概念研究为设计，以非操作性为边界。

This research discusses structural requirements for advanced AI governance. It does not provide implementation instructions, deployment mechanisms, or actionable methods for disabling, attacking, bypassing, or compromising real systems.

本研究讨论高级 AI 治理的结构要求，不提供实现步骤、部署机制，或任何攻击、绕过、破坏现实系统的可操作方法。

09 / What happens next09 / 下一步

From a striking proposition to a research contribution.

从一个引人注目的命题，走向真正的研究贡献。

This is not yet a paper. The next standard is rigor: establish novelty against the literature, define sovereignty and conditional continuity precisely, derive falsifiable claims, compare alternatives, and expose the framework to its strongest objections.

这还不是一篇论文。下一步的标准是严谨：在文献中证明新意，精确定义主权与条件化连续性，提出可证伪命题，比较替代方案，并让框架接受最强反驳。

  1. Map the relevant literature and state the exact gap.建立相关文献地图，并明确指出理论缺口。
  2. Formalize the separation between capability, agency, authority, and legitimacy.形式化区分能力、行动性、权威与合法性。
  3. Derive criteria that could show the theory to be wrong.提出能够证明理论错误的判定标准。
  4. Compare race, slowdown, and non-sovereign configurations across both outcome scales.在人类存续与社会经济主体性两个尺度上，比较竞速、减速与非主权结构。
  5. Stress-test mirror deterrence and institutional constraints against the strongest objections.用最强反驳检验镜像威慑与制度约束。
